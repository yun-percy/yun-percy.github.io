<td id="article_content"><p><strong>echo</strong>是一种最常用的与广泛使用的内置于Linux的bash和C shell的命令，通常用在脚本语言和批处理文件中来在标准输出或者文件中显示一行文本或者字符串。</p>
<p><img src="http://img.linux.net.cn/data/attachment/album/201410/03/210844zi1h0ni0ij10tkdi.gif" alt=""></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>echo命令的语法是：</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">echo </span><span class="pun">[选项]</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="pun">[字符串]</span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_1"><strong>1.</strong> 输入一行文本并显示在标准输出上</h3>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> a community of </span><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="typ">Nerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<p>会输出下面的文本:</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> a community of </span><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="typ">Nerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_2"><strong>2.</strong> 输出一个声明的变量值</h3>
<p>比如，声明变量<strong>x</strong>并给它赋值为<strong>10</strong>。</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln"> $ x</span><span class="pun">=</span><span class="lit">10</span></li></ol></pre>
<p>会输出它的值：</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="typ">The</span><span class="pln"> value of variable x </span><span class="pun">=</span><span class="pln"> $x </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="typ">The</span><span class="pln"> value of variable x </span><span class="pun">=</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="lit">10</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_3"><strong>3.</strong> 使用‘<strong>\b</strong>‘选项</h3>
<p>‘<strong>-e</strong>‘后带上'\b'会删除字符间的所有空格。</p>
<p><strong>注意：</strong> Linux中的选项‘<strong>-e</strong>‘扮演了转义字符反斜线的翻译器。</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"Tecmint \bis \ba \bcommunity \bof \bLinux \bNerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="typ">TecmintisacommunityofLinuxNerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_4"><strong>4.</strong> 使用‘<strong>\n</strong>‘选项</h3>
<p>‘<strong>-e</strong>‘后面的带上‘\n’行会在遇到的地方作为新的一行</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"Tecmint \nis \na \ncommunity \nof \nLinux \nNerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L3"><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L4"><span class="pln">a </span></li><li class="L5"><span class="pln">community </span></li><li class="L6"><span class="pln">of </span></li><li class="L7"><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L8"><span class="typ">Nerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_5"><strong>5.</strong> 使用‘<strong>\t</strong>‘选项</h3>
<p>‘<strong>-e</strong>‘后面跟上‘\t’会在空格间加上水平制表符。</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"Tecmint \tis \ta \tcommunity \tof \tLinux \tNerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln">     </span><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln">  a   community   of  </span><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln">   </span><span class="typ">Nerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_6"><strong>6.</strong> 也可以同时使用换行‘<strong>\n</strong>‘与水平制表符‘<strong>\t</strong>‘</h3>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"\n\tTecmint \n\tis \n\ta \n\tcommunity \n\tof \n\tLinux \n\tNerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="pln">    </span><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L3"><span class="pln">    </span><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L4"><span class="pln">    a </span></li><li class="L5"><span class="pln">    community </span></li><li class="L6"><span class="pln">    of </span></li><li class="L7"><span class="pln">    </span><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L8"><span class="pln">    </span><span class="typ">Nerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_7"><strong>7.</strong> 使用‘<strong>\v</strong>‘选项</h3>
<p>‘<strong>-e</strong>‘后面跟上‘\v’会加上垂直制表符。</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"\vTecmint \vis \va \vcommunity \vof \vLinux \vNerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L3"><span class="pln">        </span><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L4"><span class="pln">           a </span></li><li class="L5"><span class="pln">             community </span></li><li class="L6"><span class="pln">                       of </span></li><li class="L7"><span class="pln">                          </span><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L8"><span class="pln">                                </span><span class="typ">Nerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_8"><strong>8.</strong> 也可以同时使用换行‘<strong>\n</strong>‘与垂直制表符‘<strong>\v</strong>‘</h3>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"\n\vTecmint \n\vis \n\va \n\vcommunity \n\vof \n\vLinux \n\vNerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L3"><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L4"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L5"><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L6"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L7"><span class="pln">a </span></li><li class="L8"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L9"><span class="pln">community </span></li><li class="L0"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">of </span></li><li class="L2"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L3"><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L4"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L5"><span class="typ">Nerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<p><strong>注意：</strong> 你可以按照你的需求连续使用两个或者多个垂直制表符，水平制表符与换行符。</p>
<h3 id="toc_9"><strong>9.</strong> 使用‘<strong>\r</strong>‘选项</h3>
<p>‘<strong>-e</strong>‘后面跟上‘\r’来指定输出中的回车符。（LCTT 译注：会覆写行开头的字符）</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"Tecmint \ris a community of Linux Nerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> a community of </span><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="typ">Nerds</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_10"><strong>10.</strong> 使用‘<strong>\c</strong>‘选项</h3>
<p>‘<strong>-e</strong>‘后面跟上‘\c’会抑制输出后面的字符并且最后不会换新行。</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"Tecmint is a community \cof Linux Nerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> a community </span><span class="lit">@tecmint</span><span class="pun">:~</span><span class="pln">$ </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_11"><strong>11.</strong> ‘<strong>-n</strong>‘会在echo完后不会输出新行</h3>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">n </span><span class="str">"Tecmint is a community of Linux Nerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> a community of </span><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="typ">Nerds@tecmint</span><span class="pun">:~/</span><span class="typ">Documents$</span><span class="pln"> </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_12"><strong>12.</strong> 使用‘<strong>\a</strong>‘选项</h3>
<p>‘<strong>-e</strong>‘后面跟上‘\a’选项会听到声音警告。</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">-</span><span class="pln">e </span><span class="str">"Tecmint is a community of \aLinux Nerds"</span><span class="pln"> </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="typ">Tecmint</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="kwd">is</span><span class="pln"> a community of </span><span class="typ">Linux</span><span class="pln"> </span><span class="typ">Nerds</span></li></ol></pre>
<p><strong>注意：</strong> 在你开始前，请先检查你的音量设置。</p>

<h3 id="toc_14"><strong>14.</strong> 打印制定的文件类型</h3>
<p>比如，让我们假设你想要打印所有的‘<strong>.jpeg</strong>‘文件，使用下面的命令。</p>
<pre class="prettyprint linenums prettyprinted" style=""><ol class="linenums"><li class="L0"><span class="pln">$ echo </span><span class="pun">*.</span><span class="pln">jpeg </span></li><li class="L1"><span class="pln">&nbsp;</span></li><li class="L2"><span class="pln">network</span><span class="pun">.</span><span class="pln">jpeg </span></li></ol></pre>
<h3 id="toc_15"><strong>15.</strong> echo可以使用重定向符来输出到一个文件而不是标准输出</h3>
